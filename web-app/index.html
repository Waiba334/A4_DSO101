// Our Story

Born from a love
of honest coffee

Sépia began in a small converted warehouse in Thimphu, driven by a single question: what does truly great coffee taste like when every decision is made with care?

We work directly with smallholder farms across Bhutan, Nepal, and Ethiopia — paying above fair-trade prices and visiting every harvest season. Our roasts are slow, our extractions deliberate, and our service unhurried.
